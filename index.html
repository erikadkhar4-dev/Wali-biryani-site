The Kitchen

Dum-cooked biryani, built the Hyderabadi way

Tucked near Choudhary Gate on Barchi Road, this is a neighbourhood biryani house doing one thing with real conviction — long-grain rice, slow-cooked masala, and chicken sealed under dum until the flavour has nowhere left to go.

Alongside the biryani, the kitchen turns out tandoori grills, loaded shawarma rolls, and a full spread of chicken and mushroom starters for anyone who wants more than one dish on the table.
